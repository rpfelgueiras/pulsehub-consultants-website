How do we work?

Discovery

We would like to spend a few days with you and your leadership team to comprehensively understand how you operate your business day to day as it is today. We will do this with a mix of shadowing and interviewing your team members.

Reporting

Once we have gathered the information we will then go away as a team and discuss what is the best digital approach for your business. At this point, we will also look at any user data or user feedback you already have. We will then take you through our report, either in person or virtually.

Agreement

If you are happy with our approach and wish to proceed, we will then work with you to map out the delivery roadmap and identify all the tasks both our teams will need to complete and when to prepare for the launch of your online site.
