Our Work Process

1.

Discovery

Dedicated time is spent with you and your team to thoroughly understand your day-to-day operations. This involves observing your operations and interviewing your team members to grasp your processes and challenges.

2.

Analysis and Reporting

After gathering information, our team collaborates to analyse findings and determine the optimal digital strategy for your business. We examine existing user data or feedback to inform our approach. Following this, we present a detailed report outlining our proposed strategy and recommendations.

3.

Agreement and Project Start

Once you approve our approach, we establish a clear delivery roadmap and identify tasks for launching your site. Special needs and requirements are thoroughly addressed before initiating the project, ensuring a seamless transition into implementation.

4.

Maintenance and Account Management

Throughout the project lifecycle, we maintain open communication and provide regular updates to keep you informed of our progress. We share our progress step by step, allowing you to track development and provide feedback, ensuring alignment with your goals and preferences.
